Departments

The Institute is organized into 7 Engineering departments

  1. Computer Science and Engineering
  2. Civil Engineering
  3. Electrical-Electronics & Power Engineering
  4. Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering
  5. Information Technology
  6. Mechanical Engineering
  7. Master of Computer Application

All the departments offer full-time/part time undergraduate, fulltime/part time post graduate and research programs. Being a government institute admissions are given strictly on the basis of merits and as per the norms of AICTE & Government of Maharashtra.
